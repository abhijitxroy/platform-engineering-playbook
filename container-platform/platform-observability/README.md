

# Platform Observability

## Overview

Platform observability is the capability of understanding the health, performance, reliability, behavior, and operational state of platform infrastructure and platform services through telemetry data.

Platform engineering teams use observability to monitor platform health, troubleshoot incidents, improve reliability, optimize performance, and provide visibility into shared engineering platforms.

Platform observability is a foundational capability within platform engineering, Kubernetes platforms, cloud-native infrastructure, SRE, and large-scale distributed systems.

---

## Why Platform Observability Matters

Without Platform Observability:

```text
Platform Issue
        ↓
Limited Visibility
        ↓
Slow Troubleshooting
        ↓
Operational Risk
```

With Platform Observability:

```text
Platform Issue
        ↓
Telemetry Data
        ↓
Rapid Diagnosis
        ↓
Reliable Operations
```

Benefits:

- Faster Troubleshooting
- Improved Reliability
- Better Performance Visibility
- Reduced Downtime
- Operational Confidence
