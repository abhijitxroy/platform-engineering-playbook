

# Golden Paths

## Overview

Golden Paths are recommended, standardized, and well-supported engineering workflows that help developers build, deploy, operate, and maintain software using proven platform practices.

Platform engineering teams create Golden Paths to reduce cognitive load, improve consistency, accelerate onboarding, increase platform adoption, and guide teams toward secure and reliable engineering practices.

Golden Paths are a foundational capability within platform engineering, internal developer platforms, developer experience programs, and modern engineering organizations.

---

## Why Golden Paths Matter

Without Golden Paths:

```text
Developers
        ↓
Many Technology Choices
        ↓
Inconsistent Workflows
        ↓
Operational Complexity
```

With Golden Paths:

```text
Developers
        ↓
Recommended Workflow
        ↓
Platform Standards
        ↓
Reliable Delivery
```

Benefits:

- Faster Onboarding
- Reduced Cognitive Load
- Consistent Engineering Practices
- Improved Reliability
- Higher Platform Adoption

---

## Core Golden Path Principles

### Opinionated Defaults

Provide recommended approaches for common engineering tasks.

Examples:

- Application Templates
- CI/CD Templates
- Deployment Standards

---

### Self-Service Enablement

Developers should be able to follow the path without platform team intervention.

Examples:

- Automated Provisioning
- Deployment Automation
- Platform Workflows

---

### Security And Compliance

Golden Paths should include approved security and governance controls.

Examples:

- Security Scanning
- Policy Validation
- Compliance Checks

---

### Continuous Improvement

Golden Paths evolve as platform capabilities mature.

Examples:

- Workflow Optimization
- Developer Feedback
- Platform Enhancements
