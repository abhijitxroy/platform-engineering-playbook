

# Platform Adoption

## Overview

Platform adoption is the process of encouraging, measuring, and increasing the usage of platform capabilities, self-service workflows, engineering standards, and internal developer platforms across engineering organizations.

Platform engineering teams focus on platform adoption because even the best platform provides limited value if developers do not actively use it.

Platform adoption is a foundational capability within platform engineering, developer experience programs, internal developer platforms, and engineering productivity initiatives.

---

## Why Platform Adoption Matters

Without Platform Adoption:

```text
Platform Investment
        ↓
Low Usage
        ↓
Limited Value
        ↓
Poor Outcomes
```

With Platform Adoption:

```text
Platform Investment
        ↓
Developer Usage
        ↓
Standardized Workflows
        ↓
Improved Productivity
```

Benefits:

- Higher Engineering Productivity
- Better Standardization
- Improved Developer Experience
- Greater Platform ROI
- Faster Software Delivery

---

## Core Adoption Drivers

### Developer Experience

Developers adopt platforms that make their work easier.

Examples:

- Simple Workflows
- Better Documentation
- Reduced Complexity
- Self-Service Capabilities

---

### Platform Value

The platform must solve real engineering problems.

Examples:

- Faster Deployments
- Easier Environment Provisioning
- Reduced Operational Overhead

---

### Platform Reliability

Developers trust platforms that are stable and reliable.

Examples:

- Consistent Performance
- Reliable Automation
- Strong Support Models

---

### Communication And Enablement

Platform teams must educate users.

Examples:

- Training Sessions
- Adoption Guides
- Developer Feedback Programs

## Platform Engineering Perspective

Platform teams commonly improve adoption through:

- Developer Portals
- Golden Paths
- Self-Service Platforms
- Documentation
- Training Programs
- Feedback Loops

The goal is making platform usage the easiest and most valuable choice.

## Production Challenges

Common challenges include:

- Resistance To Change
- Poor User Experience
- Limited Platform Awareness
- Missing Documentation
- Excessive Complexity
- Low Trust In Platform Reliability

Successful platform teams treat adoption as a product and user experience problem.
